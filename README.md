# Vrinda_store_dataAnalysis_practice_excel

Executive Summary of Insights : 

The data reveals a highly specific, high-performing customer profile and clear geographic and channel preferences for Vrinda store sales.

🔍 Key Data Points:

Demographic Breakdown:

   Gender: Women are the primary buyers, driving approximately 65% of sales.
   Age: The adult age group between 30–49 years is the highest contributor, accounting for roughly 50% of total sales.
   

Sales Channels:

Top Platforms: Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra are the maximum contributing channels for sales volume.

🎯 Actionable Final Conclusion

To maximize ROI and increase Vrinda store sales, marketing efforts should be hyper-targeted rather than broad.

Strategic Recommendation

Target Audience: Target women aged 30–49 years.

Location: Focus campaigns exclusively on residents of Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh.

Execution: Deploy targeted ads, exclusive offers, and discount coupons specifically through Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra to meet the audience where they already shop.
